The Tropical Cyclone Monthly Bulletin

Since January 1993 the Forecasting Systems branch of the UK Met. Office has issued a document entitled "Monthly Summary of Tropical Cyclone Activity and Forecasts" which is sent to many individuals and meteorological centres around the globe with an interest in the forecasting of tropical cyclones. It contains information on the month's tropical cyclone tracks and statistics and charts relating to the performance of the UKMO global model in forecasting the track of each storm.
