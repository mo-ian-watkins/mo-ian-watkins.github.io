International

The Met.Office's international services vary from forecasts for overseas television companies to training potential forecasters for other National Met. Services.

The majority of services offered by The Met.Office in the UK are also available for overseas sites. Thus we provide forecasts to Formula 1 Grand Prix teams as they travel around the world and to coffee growers in Brazil.

Developing countries may not have the infrastructure of observers and Weather Centres that we have in the UK but we welcome the opportunity to collaborate and co-operate in setting up and establishing the necessary computer and communication systems for running an effective meteorological service.
