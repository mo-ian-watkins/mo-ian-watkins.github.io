Insurance

Insurance companies need to be able to settle claims quickly and effectively. The Met.Office, through its Weather Proof service, can provide a comprehensive service in terms of weather data direct to PC or via fax to enable weather related claims, such as household damage, to be settled without time and money consuming disputes.

The Weather Proof service can be adapted to individual insurance companies by area and time and is available as Electronic Proof direct to PC's or Basic Proof in hard copy format.
