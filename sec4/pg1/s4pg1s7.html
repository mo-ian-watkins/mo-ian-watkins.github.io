Environment

The Met.Office is a world leader in the modelling of the atmosphere. These models can be used by Local Authorities, Industrial Plants and many other organisations to predict both short and long range dispersions of atmospheric pollutants. Thus ensuring the most effective measures are taken. The pollutants monitored can include noise, chemicals, gases etc.

Individual studies are undertaken by our consultancy branch Metstar.

Through its MORECS data base The Met.Office can offer information on soil moisture content and thus offer a service to landfill operators which saves time and money in day to day operations.

Long term effects on the climate due to the activities of man are studied by The Hadley Centre part of The Met.Office which is funded by the Department of the Environment.

Through the Public Met. Service, The Met.Office provides emergency services to the police and the Atomic Energy Authority. Thus in the case of a chemical spillage The Met.Office through its Chemet service will provide the local police with the necessary information for public warnings and evacuations.
