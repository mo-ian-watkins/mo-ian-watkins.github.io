The Weather Initiative

The Weather Initiative (TWI) is a business unit in The Met.Office dedicated to serving the interests of manufacturing and retailing in the UK and overseas.

Using some of the most advanced forecasting techniques in the world, the aim is to help businesses become more profitable by relating company performance to weather data and geography.

This process of Weather Sensitivity Analysis enables our customers, such as supermarkets and motor component manufacturers, improve their stock control, planning of manpower and production, raw material sourcing and many other critical business processes. The end result is increased profitability.
