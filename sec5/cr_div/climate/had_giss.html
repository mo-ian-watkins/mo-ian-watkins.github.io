GISST Global Ice and SST data

Description:

Monthly mean SST and ice coverage from 1948 to 1991. Global cover - 1deg x 1deg grid - PP format.

Comment:

Errors in Hudson Bay and ice-edge area in South East Pacific.

References:

Martinson D. G. et al. (editors) 1994:

Natural Climate Variability on Decade-to-Century Time Scales. National Academy Press, Washington DC, USA.

Parker, D. E., Folland, C. K., Bevan, A., Ward, M. N., Jackson, M. & Maskell, K. 1992:

Marine Surface Data for Analysis of Climatic Fluctuations of Interannual to Century Timescales. National Research Council (National Academy of Sciences) Paper Presented at the Workshop on Climate Variability on Decade-to-Century Timescales. Irvine, California, USA.
