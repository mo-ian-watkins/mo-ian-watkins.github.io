Digital Chart of the World (DCW)

Description:

A new and comprehensive 1:1,000,000 vector base map of the world. Elements included are coastline, 1,000ft contours, spot heights, drainage and limited vegetation details.

Reference:

The DCW Product Specification (MIL/D/89009) for Defence Mapping Agency Systems Centre (AQE), 8613, Lee Highway, Fairfax, Virginia.

Comment:

Raw data are currently available. Met. Office are converting to a more useful form.

Location:

CD-ROM - Met O (CC) Hadley Centre
