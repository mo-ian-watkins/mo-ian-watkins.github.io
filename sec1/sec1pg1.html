Welcome to The Met. Office on the Internet!

In the UK, everyone talks about the weather. Moreover, everyone is affected by the ever-changing British weather, to some extent. And in the UK, The Met. Office, the national weather service, is synonymous with the weather.

As a major user of computer technology, I am delighted that The Met. Office has a place on the Internet. Through this medium, we have an excellent opportunity to explain and demonstrate the science of meteorology and the way in which we apply it for the benefit of the nation.

The Met. Office is an organization with a tremendous depth of experience and a wide range of responsibilities. From research to operational services, from agriculture to aviation, from flood to drought, The Met. Office provides an invaluable service to the British people. Moreover, on the international stage, our staff make huge contributions.

Our site is in the early stages of its development and we expect to provide much more information over the coming months. For us at The Met. Office, the weather is a fascinating subject. We think that many, many people think so too and we would like your help in developing our site. If there is some aspect of meteorology that you would like to know more about, or feel that there is something we have "missed out", please let us know. We'll see what we can do!

In the meantime, I hope you enjoy your visit to The Met. Office on the Internet!

Professor Julian Hunt
Chief Executive
