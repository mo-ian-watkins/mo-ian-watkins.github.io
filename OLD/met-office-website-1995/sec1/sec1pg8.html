The Met. Office College

The Met. Office College is based at Shinfield Park, near Reading. It has residential accommodation as well as high standard training facilities. The College is a world leader in the provision of meteorological training, attracting students from many parts of the world to its courses. Each year about 100 overseas students from 30 different countries are trained at the College or under its auspices.

The primary role of the College is to provide training to Met. Office staff. Normally, scientific and technical staff undergo training at the College at least every six years. In a typical year there are about 50 courses for R&D staff, forecasters, observers and others for whom a knowledge of meteorology is useful. In addition there are about 30 courses for technicians and computing staff.

Developments in technology and the need to satisfy the requirements of customers has increased the range of skills required by staff in The Met. Office. For example, core forecasting courses now put much greater emphasis on the interpretation of numerical prediction models and satellite imagery, and the way in which forecast information is communicated to customers.

Training requirements are forever changing. Therefore the College has to take the lead in identifying training needs and finding ways in which they can be satisfied. This has led to the continuous evolution of courses and the development of new training materials and techniques. In particular, the College is taking part in a major project concerned with the development of computer-aided learning modules for professional meteorologists within Europe.

In addition to the training given to Met. Office staff and those from other National Meteorological Services, the College provides a number of courses each year for interested amateurs. These are aimed at auxiliary or climatological observers - people who are not employed by The Met. Office but who provide observations at certain times of the day or even simply at the end of each month. Additional specialized courses are run for groups such as aviators, sailors and teachers.

As you can see, the work of The Met. Office College covers a wide range, far greater than that outlined here. If you would like to know more about The Met. Office College and its work, please contact us at rwriddaway@meto.govt.uk
