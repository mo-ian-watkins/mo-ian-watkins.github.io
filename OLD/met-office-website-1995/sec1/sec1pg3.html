The Met. Office - Where we are

The operational centre of The Met. Office is located at Bracknell in Berkshire, the site also of our main administrative Headquarters and major research facilities.

We operate a network of weather observing sites around the UK, many of which are located on airfields. While the majority of these are manned, we are making increasing use of automated observing systems, especially in more remote areas.

We also operate two networks of forecasting offices, one specializing in the provision of information to the armed forces, especially the RAF, and the other focusing on services to industry, commerce, including the media, and the public. Our TV/radio weather presenters are probably familiar to most people!

The Met. Office maintains a significant research centre at Cardington in Bedfordshire. Also, a joint research facility with the University of Reading is located at Reading in Berkshire.
