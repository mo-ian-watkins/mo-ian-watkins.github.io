Aviation - Commercial

The Met.Office provides services to the Civil Aviation Authority (CAA), which is the UK Met. Authority for Aviation, and provides tailored services to commercial organisations world-wide.

Civil Aviation Authority

The Met.Office is one of only two World Area Forecast Centres appointed by the International Civil Aviation Organisation (ICAO) through the British government and the CAA, providing global forecasts of upper winds and temperatures for all flights throughout the world.

Under the direction of the CAA The Met.Office provides low level forecasts for aviation over the UK and offshore waters. A basic service agreed with the CAA provides information to enable all pilots, whether flying for pleasure or for commercial benefits to self brief before take off.

Aerodrome landing forecasts (TAFS) are provided as are (METARS)
