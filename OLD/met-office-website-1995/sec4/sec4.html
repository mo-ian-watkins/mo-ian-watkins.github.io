Services to the Public

For general weather forecasts in the UK there are the Weathercall telephone service and the MetFax dial-up fax service.
For specific outdoor and sporting activities there are the Marinecall and MetFax Marine services for the wide range of Leisure Marine sports and pastimes, MetFax Aviation for those taking to the air and MetCALL Mountain and MetFAX Mountain for walkers and climbers. For further information about using MetFAX, click here

All these telephone and fax services are charged at the premium rate: 39p per minute cheap rate and 49p per minute at other times.

Services to Business

The Services and Business Division of The Met.Office has been set up to meet the needs of the many and various customers who use our weather services in the UK and overseas.

The Met.Office offers the most accurate and best supported weather service in the UK using the combination of superior computing power (one of the largest in Europe) and local Weather Centres.

The Met.Office provides specialised tailored weather services to areas of commercial and military activity in the UK and overseas.
