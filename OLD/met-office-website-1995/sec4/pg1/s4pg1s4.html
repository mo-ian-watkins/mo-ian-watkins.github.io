Building and Construction

A range of services are offered under the Sitewise heading enabling architects, engineers and contractors to design, plan and build in a cost effective manner.

For architects the climate of the site is important in getting design details correct, for engineers and contractors it is important to have climate and forecast information to enable planning and scheduling to be done effectively.

The Met.Office provides site specific weather information for all these applications. Each project is different and many products are tailored individually but amongst the routine services are the following:

    MetBuild Downtime Averages These invaluable data give for any site the number of days, according to the time of year, when inclement weather is likely to affect day to day operations on a construction project.

    MetBuild Downtime Summaries These extremely useful data give the number of days in any month when inclement weather would have affected project operations on a particular site.

These products are of relevance not only to the project designers and constructors but also to the owners and operators such as Water companies and Highway engineers.

There are also specialised services for those in the marine sector involved in coastal construction and ship building where other factors such as wave heights and storm tides become relevant.
