Water

The water industry has a challenging task to provide potable water and disposal services to a wide variety of users throughout the UK. The Met.Office provides services to all parts of the industry from water suppliers to sewage engineers. In this industry the weather affects both the demand for the product and the availability of supplies thus it is imperative that the operating companies have an accurate site specific service tailored for their needs.

A range of services is available:

    MIST a system of providing current weather information direct to your PC.
    Weather Sensitivity Analysis a process for relating business variables to the weather.
    Forecasts available in a variety of timescales.
    MORECS a data based system giving soil moisture content and thus the relationship between rainfall and available water.
    Rainfall Programmes
