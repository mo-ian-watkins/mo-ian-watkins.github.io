Marine Inshore

Knowledge of the weather forecast is vital to the safety of people involved in the wide range of sporting and commercial activities that take place in our inshore waters. The Met.Office provides services both to the sports person and to the operators of sporting and commercial facilities. These services are also designed to help the individual maximise their enjoyment and the operator maximise their cost effectiveness.

The range of products include the telephone weather service Marinecall and the MetFAX Marine fax service. For more specialist advice there is a telephone consultancy service which can be arranged on a repayment basis, tel. 01703 220646 and MIST, a system which provides customers with the latest weather information on a PC. The Inshore Waters, Offshore Waters forecasts and Gale Warnings are copied to this site.

For a copy of 'Services to Leisure Marine' - a booklet which gives details of all the Met. Office's services covering inshore waters and other useful information, please fax your address and details to 01344 85854018.
