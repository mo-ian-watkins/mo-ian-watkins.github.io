The Land Transport Group

The Land Transport Group is a business unit within The Met.Office based in Birmingham. The aim of the group is to help its customers provide an excellent smooth running service to users of road, rail and air with the least possible impact from the weather.

OpenRoad is a service specifically for those responsible for highway maintenance such as local authorities. In conjunction with Vaisala TMI Ltd a highly accurate service is provided involving interpretation of data from specifically positioned sensors. The service includes a range of forecasts from specific ice prediction and 24 hour forecasts to 2-5 day and 6-10 forecasts. Direct telephone access is provided 24 hours a day to a forecaster at a local Weather Centre.

OpenRail is a service for those providing railway transport to commerce and the general public. It includes as well as routine forecasts specialised services for ice, snow, excess heat and leaves. There are also special products for steam train operators and those concerned about keeping platforms safe.

Other services offered by the Land Transport Group include OpenFreight, OpenRunway and OpenBridge all tailored to the specific needs of particular transport operators.
