Consultancy

The Met.Office, through its internationally renowned Consultancy Metstar, offers a vast range of meteorological Consultancy services covering a variety of topics from public health to software packages for other National Met. Services.

Topics covered include:
The relationship between asthma and thunderstorms.
Flood forecasting
Dam design in relation to maximum precipitation
Prison suicide rates and the connection with the weather.

In addition the sophisticated Numerical Weather Prediction models used by Metstar are available under licence.

Customers, sponsors and collaborators include: National Rivers Authority, Institute of Hydrology, Romanian National Met. Service, Mexican National Met. Service, Commission of the European Communities Directorate, University of Salford, University of Essex etc.
