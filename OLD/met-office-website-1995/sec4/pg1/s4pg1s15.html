Sport

The weather affects almost every aspect of outdoor sport, and accurate and routine weather information can be of great benefit to sports stadiums and clubs whether it be for a full season or for major competitions. At the very worst, inclement weather can completely disrupt even the most prestigious sporting event, and over a full season the routine use of weather information will bring financial savings.

For those responsible for ground or pitch maintenance, the Met.Office can provide information on expected rainfall leading to more efficient water use in preparing playing surfaces, and a more rapid response to changing weather during play. Our temperature and frost forecasts enable more effective use of under-soil heating giving significant reductions in energy bills.

Routine weather forecasts can also be of benefit in anticipating attendance numbers for crowd management purposes, safety, catering etc.

For international travel, we can provide climatological information enabling you to anticipate likely weather conditions and plan accordingly, whilst nearer the time our forecast service will allow you to finalise your tactics and travel arrangements.

The Met Office already provides services to major tennis and golf tournaments, football clubs and racecourses, even Formula One racing teams and climbers at the top of Everest.
