Legal

Solicitors are often faced with legal cases that involve the weather, be it motor accidents or household damage disputes with insurance companies. The Met.Office through its Weather Evidence service can provide legal reports and expert witnesses to ensure that cases are settled as quickly and cost effectively as possible. We also have an emergency service to ensure that reports when urgent are received as quickly as possible. In addition there is a telephone consultancy service which can advise whether the full report or witness is necessary.
