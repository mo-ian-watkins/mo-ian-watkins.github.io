Agriculture

To help the agricultural community plan day to day activities, such as harvesting and irrigation, in a cost effective manner The Met.Office offers a range of up to date, site specific products and services.

The Met.Office together with ADAS maintains the National Agricultural Met. Unit at Wolverhampton which provides services to the farming community. Services are also provided directly to farmers and horticulturists through The Met.Office network of local Weather Centres and a service called Weatherfax.

Our PC weather system, MIST ,enables farmers to have current animated weather information downloaded onto their own computer at home or office.

Agrochemical companies and other agricultural organisations are also provided with specific Consultancy and meteorological data services through the Market Sector Manager at Bracknell.
