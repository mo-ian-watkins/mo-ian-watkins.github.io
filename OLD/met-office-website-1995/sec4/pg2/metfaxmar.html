MetFAX Marine

MetFAX Marine is the specialist premium rate dial-up fax weather service for sailors and other marine users, providing forecasts for 16 inshore areas around the UK and 6 offshore areas, together with the Shipping Forecast, surface pressure charts and satellite images.

For full detail of the service dial-up the MetFAX Marine Index Page on 0336 400 401, or contact the MetFAX Helpline 01344 854435 (tel) or 01344 854018 (fax) to request your free copy of our "Weather Services to Leisure Marine" booklet. Please mention that you saw this on our Internet site.

Please note that all calls are charged at 39p per minute cheap rate, 49p per minute at all other times. MetFAX products take between 2 and 5 minutes to transmit.
