Marinecall

Marinecall is a specialist premium rate telephone service giving weather forecasts for inshore waters around the UK.

For further details contact the MetFAX Helpline 01344 854435 (tel) or 01344 854018 (fax) to request your free copy of our "Weather Services to Leisure Marine" booklet.

Calls cost 39p per minute cheap rate, 49p per minute at all other times.

Weathercall is operated by Telephone Information Services, Avalon House, 57-63 Scrutton Street, LONDON, EC2A 4P, Tel: 0171 613 500
