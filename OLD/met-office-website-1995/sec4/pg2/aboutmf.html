About MetFAX

MetFAX is the premium rate dial-up fax weather service operated by the Met Office since 1991 which allows any fax machine and most fax software to receive a variety of weather services by simply dialling one of the MetFAX product fax numbers. The service operates on the principle of POLL RECEIVE whereby a fax machine can call another fax machine and request a document which is stored in its memory. In the case of MetFAX all the documents are stored in a specialised computer fax database with 90 telephone lines to cater for thousands of calls per day.

To use MetFAX from a fax machine, callers should dial the appropriate number from the handset of their fax machine, and press the start button after the fax tone. Users without a handset should follow the instructions for POLL RECEIVE in the fax handbook.

To use MetFAX from PC fax software usually requires a telephone in parallel with the modem. The fax software should be started and set to manual receive mode, then the MetFAX number dialled from the telephone. On hearing the fax tone the manual receive function should be selected, whereupon the fax software should take over the call and the telephone handset can be replaced. This technique will not work with all software, and many software packages fail to receive all MetFAX pages. Specific fax software which does work is Delrina WinFAX Pro which has inbuilt polling (called RETRIEVE REMOTE) and does not require a parallel telephone. Windows 95 has inbuilt fax retrieval software which can be found in the ACCESSORIES/FAX program group.

You are invited to try out the MetFAX system by dialling or main Index Page on 0336 400 402 where you will see details of many of the services we provide.

If you experience difficulties using MetFAX please contact the MetFAX Helpline on 01344 854435 (tel) or 01344 854018 (fax). We have details of most fax machines and software and can usually solve your problem in seconds. We hope to have an email address soon.

Unfortunately MetFAX can only be accessed from within the UK. Overseas users who are interested in accessing the service should contact the MetFAX Helpline for details of our subscription service.

Please note that all calls are charged at 39p per minute cheap rate, 49p per minute at all other times. MetFAX products take between 2 and 5 minutes to transmit.
