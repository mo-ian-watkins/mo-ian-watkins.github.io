MetCALL Mountain

MetCALL Mountain is a specialist premium rate telephone service giving mountain weather conditions for the following areas of the UK: 
 
West Highlands  0891 500 441
Trossachs, Argyll, Lochaber, NW Highlands and Skye


East Highlands  0891 500 442
Grampians East of Rannoch Moor and Cairngorms

 
Snowdonia       0891 500 449
Scottish services include information on snow and avalanche conditions.

Calls cost 39p per minute cheap rate, 49p per minute at all other times. The Met. Office, Sutton House, London Road, Bracknell, Berks. RG12 2SY. United Kingdom.
