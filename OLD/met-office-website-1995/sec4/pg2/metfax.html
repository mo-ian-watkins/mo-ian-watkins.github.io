MetFAX

MetFAX is the general premium rate dial-up fax weather service, providing forecasts for 9 areas of the UK up to 5 days ahead. Also available is a National Forecast, and other charts and satellite images

The following areas are available: 

Southeast England  0336 400 411
Southwest England  0336 400 412
Wales              0336 400 413
Midlands           0336 400 414
East Anglia        0336 400 415
Northwest England  0336 400 416
Northeast England  0336 400 417
Scotland           0336 400 418
Northern Ireland   0336 400 419
National Forecast  0336 400 410
Satellite Image    0336 400 429

The main MetFAX Index Page is on 0336 400 402

Please note that all calls are charged at 39p per minute cheap rate, 49p per minute at all other times. MetFAX products take between 2 and 5 minutes to transmit.
