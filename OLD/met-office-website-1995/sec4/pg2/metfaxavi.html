MetFAX Aviation

MetFAX Aviation is the specialist premium rate dial-up fax weather service for pilots and other aviation users provided by the Met Office in conjunction with the CAA. Products available include surface charts, significant weather charts, spot winds, planning forecasts, European charts, AIRMET forecasts, TAFs and METARs.

For full detail of the service dial-up the MetFAX Aviation Index Page on 0336 400 501, or contact the MetFAX Helpline 01344 854435 (tel) or 01344 854018 (fax) to request your free laminated MetFAX Aviation Index card. Please mention that you saw this on our Internet site.

Please note that all calls are charged at 39p per minute cheap rate, 49p per minute at all other times. MetFAX products take between 2 and 5 minutes to transmit.
