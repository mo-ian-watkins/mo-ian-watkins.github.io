Up to three leaflets are FREE. Four to ten leaflets are 1.75 (pounds sterling). Ten to seventeen leaflets are 3.50 (pounds sterling). Seventeen and over are 5.00 (pounds sterling). The scientific leaflets pack contains all the Scientific leaflets and The Met. Office brochures together with a selection of material suitable for project work. Priced at 6.00 (pounds sterling).

Climatological Memoranda for England and Wales

These summarise the characteristics of the main weather elements - Temperature, Sunshine, Rainfall, Snow, Thunder, Hail, Visibility, Humidity and Wind - in specified areas. They are not available for separate Scottish regions. For detailed information on Scotland please contact the Edinburgh Met. Office: Edinburgh Climate Section, Saughton House, Broomhouse Drive, Edinburgh, EH11 3XQ.
