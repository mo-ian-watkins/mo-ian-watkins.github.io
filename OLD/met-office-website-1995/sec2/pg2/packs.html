School Packs - Interactive and informative

Our two School Packs contain everything you need to offer pupils a challenging introduction to the weather.

'The Weather Story' is for pupils at Key Stage 1. With its bright colours and easy-to-follow themes, it encourages children to investigate the environment in which they live.

  • Work Cards - Double-sided activity based work cards with project suggestions. Make as many photocopies of these as you like for classroom use.
  • Teachers' Notes - explain the aims of the resource and provide further background information.
  • Interactive wallchart - complete with colourful removable stickers.
  • Pack of weather cards - for 'snap'-style games or pairing.

'The Weather Machine' is linked to Key Stage 2 of the National curriculum. This pack aims to develop a broader understanding of the weather, introducing children to European weather patterns and important environmental issues. Lively illustrations and carefully structured practical work encourages children to record their own measurements and to design, build and test simple instruments.

  • Work Cards - Double-sided work cards, which can be photocopied with information and project work.
  • Large weather chart illustrating Europe - Use the accompanying weather symbols to present the weather each day.
  • 'The Weather Journey' - Board game for up to four children.
  • Teachers Notes - explain the aims of the resource and provide further background information.

Weather Video and Weather Teacher's Pack

In conjunction with the BBC, The Met. Office has produced a modular video which ranges from showing the basic weather concepts such as observation and data recording to more sophisticated ideas such as remote sensing and the use of synoptic charts. Suitable for Key stages 3 and 4

The 'Weather Teacher's Resource Pack' is included with the video, but is also available as a stand-alone resource containing:

  • 11 A3-sized work sheets, which can be photocopied
  • A 24-page booklet of teacher's notes
  • A satellite image to use with work cards
