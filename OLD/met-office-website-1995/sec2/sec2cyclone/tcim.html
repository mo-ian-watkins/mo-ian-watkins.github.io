Tropical Cyclone images and movies

This page contains a selection of images of tropical cyclones and the damage they can cause. Most of the images have been obtained from various WWW sites over recent months. Apologies for such an Atlantic biased page, but good images of storms in other areas are much harder to come by. GIF and MPEG viewers are required to view the images and movies. Be warned, some are quite large so may take a while to download. Sizes are given for any over 300 Kbytes.
