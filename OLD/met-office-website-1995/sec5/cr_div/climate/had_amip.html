COLA/CAC AMIP SST and Sea Ice Data.

NB Users should contact PCMDI for details of dataset and for access to data.

Description:

Monthly values of SST and ice cover for the period Jan 1979 to Dec 1988, using all available in-situ, climatological and satellite observations. Original data are on 2deg x 2deg lat/long grid. PP format data are on 2.5deg x 3.75deg lat/long grid for period Nov 1978 to Dec 1990. Used in all AMIP model intercomparison runs.

Comment:

Poor ice values in first three years - Mike Fiorino
