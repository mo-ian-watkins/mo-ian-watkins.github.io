EQUILIBRIUM RUN

Description:

Mixed-layer Ocean and Atmosphere model, run for 10 years with 1xCO2 in the control run and 2xCO2 in the anomaly run.

Model:

2.5x3.75 degrees Latitude/Longitude grid.
11 vertical levels:-
