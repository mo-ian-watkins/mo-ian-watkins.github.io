Proudman Oceanographic Laboratory - DBDB5

NB This dataset is a copy. The Hadley Centre cannot pass the data to other users without the permission of the originators.

Description:

Digital Bathymetric Data Base - 5 minute grid - DBDB5 Ocean depths in metres for a global 1/12 degree grid. (Points corresponding to land have a value of -10.0) PP Format (64 word header)

Comment:

Particularly good resolution.

Reference:

DBDB5- A Digital Data Set of Global Gridded Bathymetry at 5 Minute Intervals.

Published by:- British Oceanographic Data Centre Proudman Oceanographic Laboratory Bidston Observatory Birkenhead Merseyside L43 7RA U.K.
