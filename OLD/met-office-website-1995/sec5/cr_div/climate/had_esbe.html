Esbensen & Kushnir multi-element data

NB This dataset is a copy. The Hadley Centre cannot pass the data to other users without the permission of the originators.

Description:

Monthly and annual values - PP format - 5deg x 4deg grid.

Comments:

The source tape is not available. It is not known whether these data are copied or interpolated from the original. Superceeded by COADS and Oberhuber, which use a different method of calculating the heat flux.

References:

Esbensen, S. K. & Kushnir, Y. 1981:

The heat budget of the global ocean: An atlas based on estimates from surface marine observations. Climatic Research Institute, Oregon State University. Report No. 29.

Grahame, N. S. 1984: Esbensen & Kushnir

climatological data available in Met. O. 20. Met. O. 20 Internal Note No. 18.
