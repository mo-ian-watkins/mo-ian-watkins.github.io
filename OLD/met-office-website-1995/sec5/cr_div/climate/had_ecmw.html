ECMWF climatology based on operational analyses

NB This dataset is a copy. The Hadley Centre cannot pass the data to other users without the permission of the originators.

Description:

Monthly and seasonal means - Monthly and seasonal climatologies - Period 1985 to 1991 - PP format - 2deg x 2deg grid. The means and climatologies have been interpolated from ECMWF spectral analysis format into PP format. Parameters - Geopotential, Temperature, Vertical Velocity, u & v wind components, Specific Humidity, Relative Humidity at 14 levels.

Comment:

The original spectral data is also available on cartridge.

Location:

ECMWF, Shinfield Met. Office Cartridge
