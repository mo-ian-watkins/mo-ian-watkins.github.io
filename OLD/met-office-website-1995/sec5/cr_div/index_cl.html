Climate data available in the Hadley Centre

AMIP
Monthly values of SST and sea ice for the period Jan 1979 to Dec 1988. Used in all AMIP model integrations.
BUNKER
Monthly Multi-element data for North Atlantic - 1deg x 1deg grid.
DBDB5
Digital Bathymetric Data Base - 5 minute grid.
DCW
A new and comprehensive 1:1,000,000 vector base map of the world.
ECMWF
Monthly and seasonal data - 2deg x 2deg grid.
EQUILIBRIUM
Mixed-layer Ocean and Atmosphere Model. Run for 10 years with 1xCO2 in the control run and 2xCO2 in the anomaly run.
ERBE
Earth Radiation Budget Experiment data. Feb 1985 to Feb 1990.
ESBEN_KUSH
Esbensen & Kushnir multi-element data.
GISST
Monthly mean SST and ice coverage from 1948 to 1991. Global cover - 1deg x 1deg grid.
HELL_ROSEN
Hellerman & Rosenstein wind stress, wind power and mixing energy.
JAEGER
Precipitation data on 5deg x 5deg grid.
LEG_WILL
Legates and Willmott precipitation and temperature climatology.
LEVITUS
Ocean potential and in-situ temperatures and salinity.
MOHMATN
Meteorological Office Marine Air Temperature (Night) Data.
MOHSST
Meteorological Office Sea Surface Temperature Data.
NDVI
Normalized Difference Vegitation Index (also known as GISS data).
NMC_ANALYSIS
NMC Vertically averaged ocean temperature from surface to 360 metres.
OA_CLIM
Met. Office Operational Analysis Archive multi-element data.
OBERHUBER
Multi-element data
OLSON
World Ecosystem Complexes.
OORT
Daily, monthly, seasonal and annual climatologies.
REGIONAL
Atmosphere only limited area model, nested within the EQUIIBRIUM model for the control and anomoly runs.
SCHUTZ_GATES
Multi-element seasonal data on 5deg x 4deg grid.
SCRIPPS
Land elevations and Ocean depths on 1deg x 1deg grid.
SHEA
Monthly, seasonal and annual Climatology on 2.5deg x2.5deg grid.
SUPERFILEPMSL
Mean sea level pressure and 500mb height data.
TRANSIENT
Coupled ocean/atmosphere model experiment.
UGAMP
UK Universities Global Atmospheric Modelling Programme Multi-element data based on ECMWF analyses.
