About the SRNWP Network

The Short-Range Numerical Weather Prediction Network is a grouping of European National Meteorological Services wishing to promote closer cooperation in the development of techniques for short-range high-resolution NWP. Cooperation is split into the following topics:

Technical

  • Establishment of a high resolution physiological database
  • Procedures and standards for exchanging data and computer code
  • Coding standards for Fortran 90 and C
  • Issues related to use of Massively Parallel Processing Computers
  • Verification

Scientific

  • 4-D variational assimilation
  • Soil/surface data assimilation
  • Non-hydrostatic models
  • Semi-lagrangian schemes
  • Soil/surface parametrization
  • Statistical "adaption" research
